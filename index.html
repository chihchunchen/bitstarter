FAQ

Why?
emotitag allows you to tag things with the emotions they evoke in you. While rating scores and votes help us to record and share our evaluations of things, they do not truly represent people's emotional responses to these things (two people might give 5 stars to the same movie for very different reasons, while one person might dislike a book for the very same reason that another person loves it). Also, emotions are often complex and a matter of degree. Users can help build a bank of emotitags by introducing new combinations of emotions with new labels. moomood allows you to make multimedia mood boards of objects, times and places, which you can share. You can also easily group together things that evoke the same moods or emotions in you and track your mood shifts over time and space.
  • They can easily retrieve things that have evoked specific emotions.
  • They can record changes in their moods over time and with changes in circumstances and context e.g. life events, travelling.
  • They can share their emotions and attitudes towards objects with others.
  • As more users emotitag an object, we obtain a far richer view of the range of emotions they are able to evoke than we would obtain from simply aggregating ratings or votes.
Where will revenue come from?
  • Highly targeted advertising and recommendation. We have an extremely rich set of data on the responses users have to specific products and the responses that are evoked by similar products. Initially, we will use affiliate programs such as amazon associates or skimlinks to obtain revenue but later on we will consider more sophisticated agreements.
  • Special data services for organisations and individuals. For example, an enterprise may want to track to responses to their products through time with demographic segmentation.
